Personal Bio

I am a Graphic Designer and Web Developer with experience in using AI technologies and WordPress to build modern professional websites. I’m passionate about visual creativity, brand identity design, and I’m also a certified ICDL trainer. Beyond the technical field, I provide social and psychological consultations to help individuals improve themselves and achieve life balance.

Vision

My vision is to merge visual creativity with modern technology to build innovative digital solutions that inspire others and create real value, while contributing to knowledge sharing and human development.

Mission

My mission is to deliver creative digital design and development solutions based on simplicity and innovation, empowering individuals through effective technology training, and supporting them socially and psychologically to become the best version of themselves.

Goals

To enhance my skills in graphic and web design using the latest tools and technologies, provide innovative digital solutions that support individuals and businesses in building a strong online presence, spread digital knowledge through ICDL and tech training, contribute to improving quality of life through social and psychological consultations, and build strong professional networks that foster collaboration and creativity.
