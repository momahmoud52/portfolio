About Me

Learn more about me, my professional journey, and my skills.

Personal Bio

I am a graphic designer and web developer with experience in using Artificial Intelligence technologies and WordPress to build modern, professional websites. I have a passion for visual creativity, brand identity design, and I am also a certified ICDL trainer. In addition to my technical work, I provide social and psychological consultations to help individuals develop themselves and achieve balance in their lives.

Vision

My vision is to merge visual creativity with modern technology to build innovative digital solutions that inspire others and deliver real value, while also contributing to knowledge sharing and human/social skills development.

Mission

My mission is to provide creative solutions in design and digital development based on simplicity and innovation, while empowering individuals to use technology effectively through training, and supporting them socially and psychologically to become the best version of themselves.

Goals

To enhance my skills in graphic and web design using the latest tools and technologies, provide innovative digital solutions that support individuals and businesses in building a strong online presence, spread technical knowledge through ICDL and digital skills training, contribute to improving quality of life through social and psychological consultations, and build a strong professional network that supports collaboration and shared creativity.
